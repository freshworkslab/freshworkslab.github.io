About the Freshworks Support Lab

Our Vision of Support Excellence

Our state-of-the-art support lab enables the Freshworks support teams to become experts in product features, resulting in improved product quality and customer satisfaction across our entire product line.

Where do we focus?

  • Freshworks Product Suite: Our lab covers all Freshworks products, including Freshdesk, Freshservice, Freshsales, and more.
  • Key Product Features: We prioritize mastering the core functionalities that drive value for our diverse customer base.
  • Quality Assurance: The lab serves as a testing ground for new features and major updates across all Freshworks products.

How do we achieve excellence?

  • Continuous Learning: We foster an environment of ongoing education and skill development for all Freshworks support team members.
  • Hands-on Experience: Our lab provides a controlled environment for experimentation and practical learning across the Freshworks ecosystem.
  • Comprehensive Testing: We conduct rigorous user acceptance testing to ensure a smooth experience for all Freshworks customers.

Through this approach, our support team gains in-depth knowledge of every aspect of the Freshworks product suite. This expertise translates directly into faster resolution times, more accurate problem-solving, and proactive identification of potential issues before they impact our valued customers.
